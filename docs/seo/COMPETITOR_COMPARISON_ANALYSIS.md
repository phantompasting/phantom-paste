## 9. Fairness & Legal Guardrails

Enforce these on every comparison page — no exceptions:

1. **Accuracy**: Every competitor claim verifiable from their public homepage as of 2026-04-22 (footnote the date on every page). Re-audit quarterly.
2. **Trademark attribution**: First mention of "Wild Posting" on any page gets the ® symbol. Never position our service as "Wild Posting."
3. **No defamation**: Acknowledge competitor strengths honestly in their win sections.
4. **Sources linked**: Every competitor data point links to their homepage or documentation.
5. **Self-disclosure**: "Phantom Pasting is the author of this comparison" disclosed in the footer of every comparison page.
6. **Pricing**: If we add pricing later, include "as of [date]" disclaimer.
7. **Update cadence**: Re-fetch and re-verify competitor data every 90 days; update `Last updated` stamp.
